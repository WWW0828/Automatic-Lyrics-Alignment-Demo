Lyrics Alignmnet

Introduction

The topic of our MIR final project is "Lyrics Alignment". First we use source seperation library Spleeter to do vocal seperation. Next we cut the seperated vocal into several 30-sec clips (each clip overlaps for 1 sec) and use Wav2Vec for speech recognition. After that, we run our algorithm to align the recognition result to the original lyrics. Finally, do force alignment with Wav2Vec and CTC algorithm and generate timestamp for each word in the lyrics.
